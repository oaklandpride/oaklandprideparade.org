  Assemblymember Buffy Wicks represents the East Bay in the California State
  Assembly, with a district spanning the communities of Oakland to Richmond.
  Asm. Wicks was recently appointed Chair of the Appropriations Committee,
  after serving as Chair of the Assembly Housing Committee for two years
  prior. Her work focuses heavily on advancing solutions to solve California's
  housing and homelessness crisis, expanding our state’s social safety net,
  protecting kids in the digital world, and championing the rights of women
  and working families. A lifelong community organizer, Asm. Wicks previously
  served on both of President Barack Obama’s campaigns, and worked for him in
  the White House. She lives in Oakland with her husband, Peter, and
  daughters, Jojo and Elly.
