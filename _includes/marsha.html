Thank you for joining us this weekend, Sunday September 7th for our 15th Annual Pride Parade!

On this page you will find your lineup number, your lineup location in the Assembly Area, and a suggested time of arrival to guarantee everyone will be present and ready to start the Parade right at 11:00 AM.

You may want to bring the following with you:

  • Comfortable footwear and socks
  • a small snack to eat while waiting
  • a bottle or two of water to make sure you are hydrated
  • any regular medications that you take
  • sunglasses and/or a hat
  • sunscreen and lip balm

Leave valuables at home, make smart choices about drinking and substance use, and pay attention to where you are and where you are going.

Above all, let's have a great Oakland Pride Day Parade and Celebration!
